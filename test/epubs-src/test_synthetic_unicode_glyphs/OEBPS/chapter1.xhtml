Inline Context

The synthetic glyphs should work inside normal prose: this sentence has a full block █, a black square ■, Greek Γεω, and turned comma ʻ in one paragraph.

They should also survive punctuation: [█] (■), Γ; ε, ω. The final word ends with a block: done█
